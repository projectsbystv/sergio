Hamburg Activities Blog

Discover the opportunities surrounding our Hamburg properties

Harbor Tour & Speicherstadt Discoveries

Available year-round • For all ages

Experience Hamburg's maritime side with a harbor tour and discover the UNESCO World Heritage Site Speicherstadt with its historic warehouses.

Highlights:
  • Harbor tours with traditional boats
  • Speicherstadt Museum and Miniatur Wunderland
  • Night photography - illuminated Speicherstadt
  • Coffee roasteries and tea trading houses

The Speicherstadt is the world's largest historic warehouse complex and offers a unique flair between tradition and modernity.

View on Google Maps

Water Sports on Inner and Outer Alster

April to October • Suitable for families

Enjoy water sports activities in the heart of Hamburg on the Alster with professional equipment and stunning city skyline views.

Activities:
  • Sailing and motorboat rental
  • Stand-up paddling with city views
  • Pedal boating on the Inner Alster
  • Alster steamer tours
  • Rowing in Alster Park

The Alster offers an oasis of relaxation in the middle of the city and numerous water sports opportunities with unique views of Hamburg's skyline.

Cultural Highlights: Elbphilharmonie & Museums

Year-round • Culture & Architecture

Immerse yourself in Hamburg's diverse cultural scene with world-famous venues and significant museums.

Cultural Highlights:
  • Elbphilharmonie - concerts & plaza visit
  • Kunsthalle Hamburg with significant collections
  • International Maritime Museum
  • Dialogue House - a different kind of experience
  • Deichtorhallen with contemporary art

Hamburg offers an impressive density of cultural institutions from historic significance to cutting-edge architecture.

Culinary Specialties: Fish Market & Schanzenviertel

Year-round • Culinary & Gourmet

Experience Hamburg's culinary diversity from the famous fish market to trendy restaurants in the hip district.

Gourmet Experiences:
  • Breakfast at Hamburg Fish Market
  • Fish sandwiches by the Elbe
  • Restaurants in the Portuguese Quarter
  • Street food markets & food trucks
  • Coffee roasteries in the Schanze

From traditional Hamburg specialties to international cuisine - Hamburg's gastronomy scene is diverse and vibrant.

Green Oases: City Parks & Gardens

March to October • Recreation & Nature

Discover Hamburg's green side in the numerous parks and gardens that offer relaxation in the heart of the city.

Green Highlights:
  • Planten un Blomen with water light concerts
  • Stadtpark with outdoor pool and planetarium
  • Old Botanical Garden
  • Jenisch Park with Elbe views
  • Ohlsdorf Cemetery as landscape park

Hamburg is one of Europe's greenest cities with over 2,500 parks and green spaces inviting you to stroll, relax, and be active.

Shopping Experiences from Traditional to Modern

Year-round • Shopping & Lifestyle

Enjoy diverse shopping experiences in Hamburg - from luxurious shopping streets to alternative trendy districts.

Shopping Highlights:
  • Mönckebergstraße with major department stores
  • Jungfernstieg with Alster Arcades
  • Schanzenviertel with individual shops
  • Fish Auction Hall with maritime products
  • Flea market at Spielbudenplatz

Hamburg offers the perfect shopping experience for every taste - from international luxury brands to unique local designers.
