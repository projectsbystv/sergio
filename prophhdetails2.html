More Info

Discover our inviting private room located in the charming outskirts of Hamburg. With peaceful surroundings and easy access to both nature and city life, this accommodation offers the perfect setting for an unforgettable stay.

The room is tastefully furnished to ensure restful nights. The spacious shared living room invites you to relax and provides a comfortable retreat after a long day. From the large balcony, you can enjoy fresh air and pleasant views while starting or ending your day in peace.

The highlight of the property is its exclusive access to a private wellness area, where you can unwind and recharge. Whether you want to relax after exploring Hamburg or simply enjoy a quiet afternoon, this space offers the perfect atmosphere.

Shops are located near the property, making it easy to get everything you need. A charming historic district is also just 1.5 km away, where you can enjoy the authentic atmosphere of Hamburg through local cafés, restaurants, and boutiques.

Wake up each morning in a calm environment, accompanied by gentle sounds of nature. This place exudes comfort and offers the ideal setting for a restful and memorable stay.
