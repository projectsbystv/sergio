More Info

Discover our charming room located in the peaceful outskirts of Hamburg. With its relaxing view over the surrounding greenery and easy access to the vibrant city center, this accommodation offers the ideal setting for an unforgettable stay.

The room is tastefully furnished to ensure restful nights. A spacious shared living area invites you to relax and provides a comfortable space to unwind. From the large balcony, you can enjoy the fresh air and a pleasant view. The balcony features a small outdoor kitchenette and a dining table, allowing you to enjoy your meals in a cozy atmosphere.

The highlight of the accommodation is undoubtedly the private wellness area. Here you can spend warm days refreshing yourself and enjoying a calm environment — the perfect place to relax after exploring the city.

Shops are located just 1.5 km away, making it easy to purchase everything you need. Additionally, a charming historic district is also just 1.5 km away, where you can discover authentic cafés, restaurants, and the unique flair of Hamburg.

Start each morning with peace and serenity, creating an atmosphere of harmony and relaxation. This place radiates comfort and offers the ideal environment for complete rest.

The accommodation includes:

  • 1 double room (incl. bed linen, towels, hairdryer, shower kit, iron, clothes rack, fridge, washing machine, dishwasher, safe, TV)
  • Shared bathroom (including shower)
  • Spacious shared living room
  • Large balcony with access
  • Outdoor kitchenette
  • Private wellness area with loungers and parasol
  • Private parking

A baby cot (125x70 up to 15 kg) and extra beds (190x80) are available on request.
